About us

KaMuseo was created by students with the goal of expressing art through a digital platform. It serves as a space where creativity can be shared and experienced. Built with creativity and passion, KaMuseo reflects how art can evolve into technology while vastyleluing the expression and culture of arts.

Mission

Mission KaMuseo aims to be a space wherein artists can freely show their art pieces while keeping the feeling of nostalgia and vibes of a real museum.

Vision

To be an online platform that encourages arts and safe spaces for artists and to be known as a digital museum. Successfully influenced the other that digital art and traditional art are both important and deserve equal recognition
